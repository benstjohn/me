I belive in the power of a process
 
 
In order to effectively and effeciently deliver my projects, I trust a methodical process to stay organized and focused. Take a look!
 
 

Listening to
your needs

First, I need you to tell me what you want! This is usually done through email.

Basic
first draft

To make sure we are on the same page, I will show you the basic draft of the site, continuing after your approval.

Polishing
the code

This is typically the longest step, and will be the majority of development. I will update you consistently.

Final
check in

Once I believe the site is finished, I will confirm the final work with you. Fixing small bugs if necessary

Delivering
the product

I will send you the finished product through a zip-file, github, or publish it to your domain.
